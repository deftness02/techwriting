# Purch

July 2010 to August 2016<br />
_Technical Writer, Freelance Content Writer_

## Job Duties

* Wrote 10-20 articles a week, averaging 6,000 words total. Writing included SEO keyword
research (density, relevance, weight, etc.) on product reviews for TopTenReviews and general
topic articles for BusinessNewsDaily, Tom’s Guide, TechNewsDaily, and LiveScience.
* Promoted to lead staff writer for a new lead-generating content website. Created more than
80% of the 300 articles included on site launch.
* Trained and mentored two new employees in the processes and writing styles needed for correct
and detailed site content.
* Contributed and adhered to online content style guide for content writing and SEO “best
practices.” This practice involved: HTML coding, content copywriting, keyword research (density,
relevance, weight, etc.), and meta tag guidelines.
* Frequently communicated with editors, SEO analysts, sales, and lead gen to maximize potential
monetization of the content.
* Performed site refreshes, which involve researching new and upcoming software products,
reviewing new competition, and revising changes to current product lineup.
* Contributed feature articles from general interest to top 10 shopping lists regarding hardware
and software technology products.
